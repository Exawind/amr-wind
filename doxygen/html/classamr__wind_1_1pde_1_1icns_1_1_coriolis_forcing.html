◆ CoriolisForcing()

amr_wind::pde::icns::CoriolisForcing::CoriolisForcing ( const CFDSim & sim)
explicit

Coriolis term for planetary rotation

Parameters are read from the abl namespace in the input file. The following parameters are available:

  • latitude: The latitude (in degrees) where the Coriolis term is computed. This argument is mandatory.
  • east_vector, north_vector (Optional) Vectors specifying the east and north directions with respect to computational domain
  • rotational_time_period Time period for planetary rotation (default: 86400 seconds)
